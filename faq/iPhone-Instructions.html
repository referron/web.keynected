Keynected Login Instructions

Some people have expressed problems logging in to Keynected.

We're currently working on more ways to login to Keynected.

In the mean time you must have an account and be signed into Facebook via the settings of your iPhone.




  • 1. First check that your internet connection is working
  • 2. Open iOS Settings (See Screenshot).
  • 3. Scroll to Facebook
  • 4. Ensure that your Facebook account is logged in here and that Keynected has not been disabled for some reason.
  • 5. Login here with your Facebook account if necessary.
  • 6. A Successful Login should show Keynected Enabled in the Facebook Settings.
